About Vinayaka Chavithi

{% if settings.about_text %}

{{ settings.about_text }}

{% else %}

Vinayaka Chavithi (Ganesh Chaturthi) is one of the most celebrated Hindu festivals, dedicated to Lord Ganesha — the elephant-headed deity of wisdom, prosperity, and good fortune. Observed with immense devotion, the festival marks the birth of Lord Ganesha and is celebrated with prayers, music, cultural programs, and community gatherings.

Our community comes together every year with great enthusiasm to install the idol, perform rituals, and celebrate the ten-day festival with joy, togetherness, and spiritual devotion. Ganesh Chaturthi is not just a religious occasion — it is a unifying cultural event that brings neighbours, families, and friends together.
